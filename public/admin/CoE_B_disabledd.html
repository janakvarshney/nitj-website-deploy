Aim and Objectives

  • Establishment of a Centre of Excellence (CoE) in 5G and Beyond at Dr. B. R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology (NIT), Jalandhar, to position the institute as a premier research and development hub for next-generation communication technologies.
  • Develop indigenous world-class Next-Generation Wireless Technologies, contributing significantly to 4G and 5G wireless standards such as 3GPP LTE, LTE-Advanced, and NR (New Radio).
  • Enhance teaching, training, and research capabilities while expanding services to industry, startups, and entrepreneurs.
  • Provide a state-of-the-art 5G testing and validation facility, enabling technology developers to perform RF, protocol, and functional verification.
  • Strengthen collaboration with leading telecom industries, startups, and research organizations to advance 5G technology adoption and innovation.
  • Enable multi-disciplinary participation by integrating electronics, AI, IoT, machine learning, cybersecurity, and automation into 5G research.
  • Foster technological self-reliance in the Indian telecom sector by contributing to Atmanirbhar Bharat (Self-Reliant India).
     

Key Objectives

  • Research & Innovation:
    • Advance 5G and Beyond technologies, focusing on FR1 (sub-6 GHz) and FR2 (mmWave bands).
    • Develop millimeter-wave (mmWave) communication systems, massive MIMO, and beamforming for efficient wireless connectivity.
    • Enable Artificial Intelligence (AI)-driven network optimization and cybersecurity solutions for secure 5G applications.
    • Investigate IoT and C-V2X (Cellular Vehicle-to-Everything) communications for smart city and automotive applications.
    • Support research in edge computing, network slicing, and private 5G networks.
  • Testing & Validation:
    • Establish a fully equipped 5G testbed for evaluating the performance, compliance, and interoperability of 5G devices.
    • Provide pre-commercialization support to startups and industries for hardware and software validation.
    • Offer 5G device certification services to meet 3GPP and national telecom regulatory standards.
  • Industry & Startup Collaboration:
    • Build long-term sustainable partnerships with telecom operators, chipset manufacturers, device makers, and software developers.
    • Offer collaborative research opportunities for joint technology development and commercialization.
    • Provide incubation support for startups developing 5G-based solutions in telecom, IoT, smart healthcare, and industrial automation.
  • Training & Skill Development:
    • Conduct cutting-edge training programs for undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) students in 5G technology.
    • Offer industry-certified courses on wireless communication, RF design, software-defined radios (SDR), and IoT integration.
    • Facilitate internship and hands-on projects with leading telecom companies.
    • Provide a remote-access learning and testing platform for distance learners and professionals.
       

Target Beneficiaries

  • Students & Researchers:
    • Undergraduate (UG) and Postgraduate (PG) students pursuing degrees in Electronics & Communication Engineering, Computer Science, AI & IoT, and other relevant fields.
    • Ph.D. scholars and postdoctoral researchers specializing in wireless communications, semiconductor technology, and embedded systems.
  • Industry & Startups:
    • Chipset manufacturers, telecom operators, and device makers requiring a 5G validation environment.
    • Indian startups and SMEs (Small and Medium Enterprises) working on 5G-enabled solutions in areas like smart cities, healthcare, industrial automation, and agriculture.
    • Automotive and IoT-based enterprises focusing on 5G-V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) and AI-driven network solutions.
  • Academia & Government Bodies:
    • Engineering institutes and universities interested in 5G curriculum development and research collaborations.
    • Regulatory authorities and policy makers involved in telecom spectrum allocation, standardization, and cyber policy.

 

The 5G Centre of Excellence (CoE) will house state-of-the-art design, simulation, and testing facilities, including:
 

  1. 5G Design & Simulation Lab
    • Capability to design & simulate antennas, MMICs (Monolithic Microwave Integrated Circuits), beamforming ICs, and massive MIMO TR (Transmit-Receive) modules.
    • Supports system-level design, 6G research, and AI-driven network optimizations.
    • Equipped with multi-domain modeling software for RF, baseband, and circuit design.
  2. 5G Testbed Infrastructure
    • A fully equipped 5G Test Bench supporting FR1 (Sub-6 GHz) and FR2 (mmWave) bands.
    • Facilities for protocol validation, conformance testing, regulatory compliance, and carrier acceptance testing.
    • Advanced real-time oscilloscopes, signal analyzers, and network analyzers for 5G waveform testing and RF validation.
  3. IoT & Wireless Communication Training Kits
    • Includes IoT sensor devices, Zigbee, LoRa, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi training modules.
    • Supports AI-powered IoT applications, edge computing, and 5G smart city use cases.
  4. RF & Microwave Circuit Design and Measurement Systems
    • Facilities for power amplifiers, filters, and oscillator circuit testing.
    • Integration with antenna pattern measurement systems.
  5. Anechoic Chamber
    • A fully shielded chamber for antenna pattern measurement and EMC/EMI testing.
    • Supports beamforming, MIMO, and radiation pattern measurements.
  6. Device Fabrication Setup
    • Capable of single and double-sided PCB prototyping.
    • Equipped with automatic tool change, drilling, engraving, and routing systems.
    • Supports fabrication of RF/microwave components.
  7. Semiconductor Parameter Analyzer
    • Advanced testing capabilities for transistors, diodes, and MMICs.
    • Provides pulse IV and CV measurements.
  8. Workstations & ESD Workbenches
    • High-performance i7 workstations for simulation and data analysis.
    • ESD-protected workbenches for electronics assembly and debugging.

 

The 5G Centre of Excellence will offer specialized training programs catering to students, researchers, and industry professionals:
 

  1. Fundamentals & Advanced Concepts
    • 5G Network Architecture, Protocols, and Deployment Strategies.
    • Massive MIMO, Beamforming, and mmWave Communication.
    • Network Slicing and Edge Computing.
    • AI and Machine Learning Applications in 5G.
  2. Hands-on Laboratory Sessions
    • Antenna and RF circuit design using simulation tools.
    • 5G device testing, spectrum analysis, and signal integrity verification.
    • Software-defined radios (SDR) and real-time protocol analysis.
  3. Industry Collaboration & Internships
    • Short-term internships with leading telecom companies and startups.
    • Joint research projects and industrial problem-solving competitions.
    • Live industry case studies on 5G deployment and network optimization.
  4. Certifications & Online Learning
    • Industry-recognized certification programs for 5G engineers.
    • Web-based learning modules with remote-access test labs.
       

Additional training programs are currently under development.
