Aim and Objectives:


The Center of Excellence (CoE) for Cybersecurity at Dr. B.R. Ambedkar National Institute of Technology Jalandhar aims to address the critical need for cybersecurity expertise in today's digital age. With the increasing risk of cyber threats associated with technological advancements, this CoE focuses on creating next-generation cybersecurity professionals equipped to tackle real-time challenges.


Key Objectives:


  1. Skill Development and Training: Providing comprehensive training for students, professionals, and minority community members in cutting-edge cybersecurity technologies.
  2. Research and Development: Establishing the infrastructure to support research scholars and postgraduate students in developing cost-effective solutions to existing cybersecurity problems.
  3. Industry Collaboration: Acting as an incubator and consultancy center for startups and small-scale industries to enhance cybersecurity capabilities.
  4. Knowledge Sharing: Engaging with faculty, students, and the public through workshops, smart classrooms, and multilingual digital platforms for widespread knowledge dissemination.

Target Beneficiaries:


  • Undergraduates and researchers seeking advanced training in cybersecurity.
  • School, ITI, polytechnic, and engineering college students
  • Startups and small-scale industries in need of consultancy and testing facilities.
  • Government agencies and local businesses requiring cybersecurity solutions.

The CoE aims to bridge the gap between industry and academia, upskill underprivileged communities, and enhance India's cybersecurity workforce to address emerging global cyber threats effectively. By fostering innovation and practical learning, it aligns with India's goals of self-reliance and digital empowerment.


  1. Advanced Cybersecurity Lab:
    Equipped with real-time simulation tools, penetration testing platforms, and advanced network monitoring systems.
  2. Core Skills Training Modules:
    Facilities designed to support hands-on learning in the following areas:
    • Ethical Hacking: Tools and techniques to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities in systems and networks.
    • Cyber Defense: Strategies to detect, respond, and recover from cyber threats effectively.
    • Web Application Security: Training on securing web applications against threats like SQL injection, XSS, and CSRF.
    • Forensics and Analysis: Advanced tools for digital forensics, malware analysis, and incident investigation.
  3. Smart Classrooms:
    State-of-the-art classrooms with video conferencing and multilingual course delivery capabilities.
  4. Research and Development Hub:
    Infrastructure for research scholars to work on innovative cybersecurity solutions, including malware analysis, threat detection, etc.
  5. Knowledge Repository:
    Access to the latest cybersecurity frameworks, case studies, and tools for learning and development.
  1. Basic to Advanced Cybersecurity Training:
    Modules covering topics from cybersecurity fundamentals to advanced threat detection and prevention.
  2. Real-time Scenario Simulations:
    Hands-on experience with real-world cybersecurity challenges like DDoS attacks, phishing, and malware analysis.
  3. Workshops for Minority Students:
    Targeted programs to enhance employability skills, free of cost for underprivileged communities.
  4. Summer Schools and Internship Programs:
    For ITI and polytechnic students, offering experiential learning and exposure to cutting-edge technologies.

    5. Additional training programs are currently under development.
